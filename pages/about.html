About Raising Roots

Empowering parents with knowledge, support, and community

Our Story

Raising Roots was born from a simple yet powerful idea: every parent deserves access to reliable information, expert guidance, and a supportive community. Founded in 2024, we've grown from a small community of parents to a comprehensive platform serving thousands of families worldwide.

Our Mission

To empower parents with the knowledge, tools, and support they need to raise healthy, happy children. We believe that by providing accessible, evidence-based information and fostering a supportive community, we can help parents navigate the beautiful journey of parenthood with confidence.

Our Vision

To create a world where every parent has access to the resources and support they need to raise thriving children. We envision a global community where parents can connect, share experiences, and learn from each other, supported by expert guidance and reliable information.

Our Values

Empathy

Understanding and supporting parents in their unique journey

Education

Providing evidence-based, reliable information

Community

Building supportive networks for parents

Trust

Maintaining the highest standards of reliability and safety
