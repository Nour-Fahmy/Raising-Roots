Home Safety Guide

Baby-Proofing Checklist

Essential safety measures for your home:

  • Install safety gates at stairs
  • Secure furniture to walls
  • Cover electrical outlets
  • Lock cabinets and drawers
  • Remove small objects and choking hazards

Room-by-Room Safety

Specific safety considerations for each room:

  • Kitchen - Keep cleaning supplies locked away
  • Bathroom - Never leave baby unattended in water
  • Living Room - Secure cords and small objects
  • Bedroom - Follow safe sleep guidelines

Car Safety Guide

Car Seat Safety

Essential car seat guidelines:

  • Use rear-facing seats until age 2
  • Install car seat properly
  • Check for recalls regularly
  • Never leave baby alone in car

Travel Safety Tips

Safe travel practices:

  • Plan for frequent stops
  • Keep emergency supplies in car
  • Check car seat temperature
  • Use window shades

Safe Sleep Practices

Safe Sleep Environment

Creating a safe sleep space:

  • Firm, flat sleep surface
  • No soft bedding or toys
  • Room sharing, not bed sharing
  • Keep room at comfortable temperature

Reducing SIDS Risk

Important safety measures:

  • Always place baby on back to sleep
  • Use a pacifier during sleep
  • Breastfeed if possible
  • Avoid smoking and alcohol
