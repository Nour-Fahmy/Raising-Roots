Baby Safety Guide

Essential information and tips for keeping your baby safe

0-3 Months Safety

  • Always use rear-facing car seat in back seat
  • Never leave baby unattended on elevated surfaces
  • Keep crib free of soft bedding, toys, and bumpers
  • Place baby on back to sleep
  • Maintain room temperature 68-72Â°F
  • Use safety straps on changing tables
  • Keep small objects out of reach
  • Install smoke and carbon monoxide detectors
  • Cover electrical outlets
  • Secure furniture to walls

3-6 Months Safety

  • Continue rear-facing car seat
  • Baby-proof home as baby becomes mobile
  • Install safety gates at stairs
  • Lock cabinets with cleaning supplies
  • Keep cords and strings out of reach
  • Use corner guards on furniture
  • Secure heavy furniture and TVs
  • Keep hot liquids away from edges
  • Use window guards or stops
  • Check toys for small parts

6-12 Months Safety

  • Maintain rear-facing car seat
  • Supervise all water activities
  • Keep bathroom doors closed
  • Use door knob covers
  • Lock medicine cabinets
  • Keep plants out of reach
  • Use stove guards
  • Install toilet locks
  • Check for lead paint
  • Keep emergency numbers posted

12-24 Months Safety

  • Keep rear-facing car seat until height/weight limit
  • Supervise outdoor play
  • Use sunscreen and protective clothing
