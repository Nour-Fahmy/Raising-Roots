Newborn Sleep Patterns

Understanding Newborn Sleep

Newborns have different sleep patterns than older babies. Here's what to expect:

  • Sleep in short cycles of 2-4 hours
  • Need to feed frequently
  • No established day/night rhythm
  • Total sleep of 14-17 hours per day

Creating a Sleep-Friendly Environment

Help your newborn sleep better with these tips:

  • Keep the room dark and quiet
  • Maintain a comfortable temperature
  • Use white noise if needed
  • Swaddle for comfort and security

Common Sleep Problems

Sleep Regression

Sleep regression is common at certain developmental stages:

  • 4-month regression - Changes in sleep cycles
  • 8-10 month regression - Separation anxiety
  • 12-month regression - Increased mobility

Managing Sleep Issues

Tips for handling common sleep challenges:

  • Maintain consistent bedtime routines
  • Create a calm sleep environment
  • Respond to your baby's needs
  • Be patient during developmental changes
