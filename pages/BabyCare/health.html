Vaccination Schedule

Important Vaccines

Essential vaccines for your baby's health:

  • Hepatitis B (Birth)
  • DTaP (2, 4, 6 months)
  • Hib (2, 4, 6 months)
  • Polio (2, 4, 6 months)
  • MMR (12-15 months)

Vaccine Safety

Understanding vaccine safety and importance:

  • Vaccines are thoroughly tested
  • Side effects are usually mild
  • Protection against serious diseases
  • Follow recommended schedule

Common Baby Illnesses

Recognizing Symptoms

Common signs of illness in babies:

  • Fever (100.4Â°F or higher)
  • Changes in feeding patterns
  • Unusual crying or fussiness
  • Changes in sleep patterns

When to Call the Doctor

Seek medical attention if your baby:

  • Has a high fever
  • Shows signs of dehydration
  • Has difficulty breathing
  • Shows unusual behavior

Baby Skincare Guide

Daily Skincare Routine

Essential steps for healthy baby skin:

  • Gentle bathing with mild soap
  • Moisturizing after bath
  • Diaper area care
  • Sun protection

Common Skin Conditions

Managing common skin issues:

  • Diaper rash - Keep area clean and dry
  • Eczema - Use gentle products and moisturize
  • Cradle cap - Gentle washing and brushing
  • Baby acne - Usually resolves on its own
